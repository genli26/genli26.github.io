About me

I am currently a research assistant (preparing for doctoral thesis work) at Department of Chemical and Metallurgical Engineering, School of Chemical Engineering, Aalto University, where I am supervised by Prof. Yongdan Li. Before that, I received the M.Eng. degree in Chemical Engineering from Xiamen University in 2021.

My research focuses on Biomass utilization, especially the conversion of lignin into chemicals with high economic value. Industrial catalysis, including photocatalysis, electrocatalysis and thermal catalysis. Theoretical simulation, density functional theory and molecular dynamics simulation.
